Chicken Aglio Olio (Main Dish)

A simple yet flavorful Italian pasta dish made with sauted garlic, olive oil, red chili flakes, and tender grilled chicken. Perfectly seasoned and served with al dente spaghetti.

Salmon Fillet (Side)

Fresh salmon fillet, lightly seasoned with salt and pepper, grilled to perfection, and drizzled with a zesty lemon butter sauce. A great source of protein and omega-3 fatty acids.

Tiramisu (Dessert)

A traditional Italian dessert featuring layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers, rich mascarpone cheese, and a dusting of cocoa powder. Creamy, slightly sweet, and full of espresso flavor.

Sparkling Water Lemonade (Drink)

A refreshing, lightly fizzy lemonade made with fresh lemons, sparkling water, and a touch of honey for natural sweetness. A perfect thirst quencher with a citrusy kick.
